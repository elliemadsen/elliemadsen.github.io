entangled

fashion@brown design team

show summary with full recording

featured in The Brown Daily Herald

entangled is a runway collection inspired by organic shapes that draw upon patterns found in nature. Webs, networks, nests, and cells become stitches, knits, tangles, and cutouts. All fabric was sourced second-hand, and many pieces were made without using a sewing machine at all. By engaging with the fabric tactilely, I imitated processes of decomposition and composition, rearranging familiar materials like rugs, bedsheets, and curtains in subversive ways.

entangled was showcased in the 12th annual Fashion @ Brown Runway Show in March 2022.
