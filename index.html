Ellie Madsen is an engineer, researcher, and designer based in New York City.

She is currently earning a master's degree in Computational Design at Columbia University Graduate School of Architecture, Preservation, and Planning, conducting research at the Center for Climate Systems Research, and teaching Hatha and Vinyasa yoga.

Ellie was born in Wisconsin, earned degrees in Computer Science and Environmental Studies from Brown University in Providence, Rhode Island, and previously worked as a software engineer at Pinterest in San Francisco, California.

You can find Ellie on GitHub, Are.na, LinkedIn, Goodreads, and email.
